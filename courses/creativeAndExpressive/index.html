welcome [489/689]

A course that investigates the development and evaluation of systems that work in concert with human beings to promote creative and expressive experiences.

  • The approaches of creative cognition, flow, and interface ecology.
  • Visual cognition and search.
  • Game logics, and their relationships to play and culture.
  • Social media and interaction.
  • Location-based technologies and systems.
  • Audio and video production for human computer interaction documentation.
  • Development and evaluation of integrative systems.

A research project-oriented course: teams and solos. Thesis development welcome. Please be focused and self-motivated.

Lectures/Labs: Tues, Thurs, 3:55-5:10,
Perceptive Sensor Networks Lab, Richardson 907.
