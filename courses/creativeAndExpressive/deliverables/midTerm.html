Develop a fully functional implementation of your proposed creative and expressive system. Implement your proposed architecture and interface. Show us what you got!
Keep principles of iterative design in mind. Get feedback from users (once your irb application is complete) and/or from members of the class whenever this will help you confirm the validity of your ideas.
Keep in mind that after spring break, your assignments are to develop a video documenting your project, and to conduct a comprensive evaluation. Work on these formdiable tasks is dependent on you having a working system . Time is of the essence! Push your research forward.
