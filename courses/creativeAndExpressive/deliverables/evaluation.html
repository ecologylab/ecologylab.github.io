evaluation
Develop and present a thorough mixed-method evaluation for your system. That is, combine quantitative and qualitative methods.
The quantitative methods must be rigorous, not just subjective evaluation Use one or more of the quantitative methods that we have discussed: protocol analysis, creative ideation, information discovery, or flow.
Also use subjective user experience data. Gather subjective quantitative data with quantized Likert scales. Gather qualitative data with open-ended questions, using questionnaires and/or semi-structured interviews. For this data, pick a balanced set of exemplars. Report trends.
Turn in 2-4 pages. Include graphs, and statistic analyses of validity.
