readings presentation

Giving a presentation on the readings means functioning as a teacher. Make yourself an expert. You must distill the significant research contributions in each readings. You must synthesize them together. Compare and contrast. Present the most important ideas. Stimulate discussion.

Of course, your powerpoint should complement what you will say. Expect your classmates to have done the readings. Show videos of the research, when they are available.
