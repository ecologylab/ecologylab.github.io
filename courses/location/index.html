welcome [489/689]

Power consumption, size, and costs of high performance computers and sensors are dropping. Multi-modal computing goes mobile. Senses of place in physical and virtual worlds are connected in mixed reality systems.

We investigate technologies, examine research, and consider social practices and culture. Students engage in building, documenting, and evaluating location-aware interactive systems.

A research project-oriented course: teams and solos. Thesis development welcome. Please be focused and self-motivated.
