evaluation
Develop and present a summative evaluation for your system. Invoke, with depth, the qualitative or quantitative methods that we've discussed in class. Collect your data thoughtfully. Develop a thorough analysis.
For a quantitative evaluations, the quantitative methods must be rigorous. Subjective evaluations quantified using Likert scales are not sufficient. Use one or more of the quantitative methods that we have discussed: creative ideation, information discovery, or protocol analysis.
Use subjective user experience data to support the harder data. Gather subjective quantitative data with quantized Likert scales. Gather qualitative data with open-ended questions, using questionnaires and/or semi-structured interviews. For this data, pick a balanced set of exemplars. Report trends.
For a qualitative analysis, use grounded theory methods. Develop codes to categorize the data.
Turn in 2-4 pages. Include graphs, and statistic analyses of validity.
You should definitely get my input on the details of your study design, as a confirmation step before you collect much data.
