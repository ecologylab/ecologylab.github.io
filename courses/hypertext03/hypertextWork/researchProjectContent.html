CPSC 610 Hypertext

Professor Andruid Kerne
research project


Your hypertext research project can be oriented toward tools and functionality, toward creativity and expression, or toward a hybrid that mixes these.

This project will be articulated through a sequence of steps:
 
  • topic due tuesday 10/20
    A 1-2 page description of what you want to address, why, and how. What are your motivations? What are your intentions, your goals? What tools do you expect to use? Create a statement of concept. End with a third page -- development plan. Each line of the plan will show one step toward accomplishing your project, and the date when you will do it.

    Be very specific.
  • activity analysis due thursday 10/30
  • initial design due tuesday 11/11
  • prototype due tuesday 11/25
  • class presentations 12/2, 12/4, 12/8
  • final implementation and report due thursday 12/11


    • Expect to do a 30 minute oral presentation, using hypermedia support materials created with one of the above research tools. Plan for (and encourage!) discussion in the midst of your presentation, as well as at the end.

    What you turn in should include text (on paper), and the hypermedia materials on cd and the web.
