welcome [csce 482]

This course weaves the strands of a computer science education into a whole, integrating algorithms, software, intelligence, systems, graphics, and information, while developing a human-centered perspective. Students will collaborate to develop exciting projects using state-of-the-art computing technologies, such as multitouch, the iPhone, web information semantics, and GPU visualization.

Senior Capstone Design emphasizes problem formation, as well as the development of solutions. You will develop research-level ideas. Projects require demonstration of methods and techniques, while providing space for you to express yourself creatively.

You will build sophisticated and reliable integrated systems of software and hardware components, through iterative design processes that take into account human needs, requirements, and imagination, as well technological characteristics and scientific methods.
