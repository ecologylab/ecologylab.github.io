Blake Dworaczyk
woezo!

spring 2004 | IE only

Woezo means, "You are welcome!" It is a greeting among the Ewe people of Ghana, when a stranger or friend comes to visit your house. This is a collection of stories about the transition to life in College Station, Texas, by international students.

Collection assembled by Babatunde Azeez, Joseph Southern, Bridgette Summerfield, and Isaac Aholu.
