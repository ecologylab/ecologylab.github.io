Conflict and Perspective
Conflict
Pick Up
 		Conflict may be thought of as a negative thing, but conflict is the only way to bring positive change. Through conflict we reformulate and improve things, concepts, and society in general. Conflict is a necessary thing to bring multi-perspective ideas together.
Nature
Pick Up
 		Many things in nature can be see as the mixture and blend of elements or characteristics whether it is for better or for worse. Humans behave in a similar way, our perspectives are created from a combination of life events, both light and dark.
Juxtaposition
Pick Up
 		Juxtaposition is a way to look at completely different perspectives or concepts at the same time, often in a visual way. In terms of conflict and perspective, it is hard to realize that other people will be affected by the choices that we make, or that they even would make a different choice in certain situations than we would.
Passion
Pick Up
 		Passions and things that you love such as family, friends, hobbies, and significant others heavily impact people's way of thinking. You make make choices that you wouldn't expect because of them.
Inner Conflict
Pick Up
 		Through inner conflict we develop our own set of values based on personal experiences and morals. People must go through some sort of internal design process when developing personal opinions, personal creations, or their own way of life.
