Juxtaposition
In Mike Figgis's Timecode, the movie shows four different camera angles at the same time. Each camera follows each different main character to show their different perspectives. This juxtaposition of perspectives is what I show here. Specifically how life events, whether positive or negative will have an effect on how people develop. We should be aware that not everyone grew up in the same way that we did.
