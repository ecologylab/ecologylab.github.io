Multi-Exploration


Welcome to the Multi-Exploration page! Here, you will be presented with two concurrent explorations starting from the same Wikipedia page. The explorations will slowly diverge as link-following decisions are made in each exploration. This results in a wide breadth of information presented and connections between disparate subjects begin to emerge.


Spanish short-story writer and essayist Jorge Luis-Borges presents a tale of contradictions and multiple realities in his work "The Garden of Forking Paths." The story's themes emphasize decisions and their diverging outcomes and he explores the idea of the "labyrinth," where subsequent decisions lead to a great number of possibilities. His ideas are displayed in the page's explorations.


Mike Figgis's experimental film "Timecode" explores a similar concept. The film follows individuals throughout a day in their lives by displaying four continuous clips simultaneously. While the viewer is confounded, struggling to make sense of the four stories occuring at once, he or she begins to see where the stories converge and interact. The user of the Multi-Exploration page is presented with information in a similar fashion and is encouraged to find these information convergences and connections as he is whisked through perhaps wildly different subjects.
