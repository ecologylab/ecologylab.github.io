Image Exploration


Welcome to the Image Exploration page. Here, you will be presented simply with images curated from Wikipedia. Each image is related to the next through a Wikipedia link.


Combining these images may result in a Rauschenburg-esqe collection of items, whose combinations of images and artifacts inspired viewers to create their own conclusions and connections. As you view the images presented, ponder the connections between the items. You may be surprised to find that they are different than the concrete connections found in Wikipedia.


The Dada art movement and well-renowned artist Marcel Duchamp championed the idea of context as it relates to art. Duchamp's "Fountain" is a great example of this, as its ordinary presentation as a urinal is unassuming. However, when presented in the context of an art exhibit and a carefully chosen name, the art piece takes on new meaning among its viewers. Similarly, the images provided here take on new meaning in the context of the other images and the connections you create.

Upon finishing your exploration, your images will be presented with their associated Wikipedia pages, allowing you to reflect on their connections further.
