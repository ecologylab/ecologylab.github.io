Sortie En Mer

Sortie En Mer is an interactive video that simulates dying due to drowning in the ocean. The user is asked to continually scroll up on the mouse wheel to keep the character afloat. As you become tired of scrolling, the character becomes tired of keeping himself above water. As you fight, flashbacks of the characters past come in and out, reflecting on the value of life, and the need to fight.

The concrete purpose of the creation of the drowning simulation is give potential boat goers an idea for how quickly you can become tired while keeping yourself afloat in the ocean. It was created by artists from Guy Cotten, a outdoor clothing manufacturer promoting safe boating equipment. With the opportunity to deliver the message, the creators were able to relate the anxiety of drowning. Although the typical user can only last several minutes, the action of continually scrolling while visually receiving information simulates the anxiety itself. Decisions to tear off clothing do not seem rational or easy to make in tough times of stress.
