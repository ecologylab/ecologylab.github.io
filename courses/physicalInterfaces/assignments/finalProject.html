final project sequence

This is an extended sequence of assignments, typically peformed by a group of 3-4 students. Each team will conceptualize, design, build, evaluate, and document an interactive system with an embodied physical interface, and author an associated research paper and video. We will be preparing submissions for the ACM Multimedia Art Program (Full Art Papers + Art Exhibitions) and the ACM CHI 2007 conference. You can see information about what was expected for CHI 2006.
