tracking and transforming video

Experience

Input Modality
Use video tracking (e.g., Jitter / Cyclops) as the basis for the human interface to an interactive work. Focus on specific qualities of human motion. What motion do you want to capture? How does it correspond to the experiential state of a human being? How will you discover this with the tools you have?

Output Modalities
The default output modality for this assignment is video. Consider the video as a medium for processing and remix.
