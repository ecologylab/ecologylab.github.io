H.R. Bright Building, Room 124

The Houston Serious Games Research Consortium, Interface Ecology Lab, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, and Department of Visualization invite all researchers, practitioners, developers, and enthusiasts to participate in the first Serious Games and Virtual Environments Day. To be held in the H.R. Bright Building, room 124 and surrounding labs, SGVE Day promises to be an exciting venue for sharing the latest research and development with the community. Presentations, demos, and lab tours will be offered. The day concludes with an informal board games meetup hosted by the Interface Ecology Lab in the Richardson Building, room 912b.
