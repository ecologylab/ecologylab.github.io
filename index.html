Texas A&M [lot 15]
     
 
The Interface Ecology Lab investigates the future of human expression, focusing on creativity, play, participation, and learning. We develop interdisciplinary research and education from our home in the Texas A&M Department of Computer Science and Engineering. The lab is directed by PI Andruid Kerne.

projects
IdeaMâché is a browser-based expressive web curation system that uses information composition as the medium of curation. Curation is the process of assembling information in meaningful exhibits, for people to think about. IdeaMâché allows users to collect, arrange, annotate and synthesize digital content to express relationships and develop ideas.

TweetBubble is a chrome extension that helps Twitter users follow associational chains of tweets through #hashtags and @users. People can experience and relate a variety of content in-context helping them develop multiple perspectives on a topic. TweetBubble makes browsing a more fun and fluid experience.
TweetBubble uses BigSemantics and Metadata In-Context Expander.
