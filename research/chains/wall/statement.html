CHAINS Grafitti Wall Statement

The Coded Messages: CHAINS grafitti wall is a community space run by the CHAINS collective under the auspices of New York University. This WWW CGI Graffitti Wall is not a public space. We maintain the space so that people can dialogue about topics like
  • African Culture
  • Post-Modernism
  • Urban Grafitti
  • Intercultural Performance
  • Marginalization
  • Potentials and limits for collaboration across boundaries of culture, race, class,...
  • Juxtapositions of Cultural Forms
The wall exists as a channel of communication to foster understanding amongst people.

The wall has been attacked by white supremacist racists, sex mongers, and other ripoff artists. These people are not welcome here. The wall is not a place to express hate, nor is it a pickup joint. If you come to the wall, and post materials not consistent with the goals of this community, you are tresspassing against our wishes, in an attempt to deny our free speech. Those who do this, you can speak where you are welcome and in public spaces as protected by the constitution. Get out of here with your garbage.
