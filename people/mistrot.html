Jean Michael Mistrot's work involves real-time data visualization, computer human interface, electronic music, interactive computer art and digital film making. He holds a Fine Arts degree in Sculpture and Printmaking from the Pennsylvania Academy of Fine Arts, as well as an M.S. in Visualization Sciences from Texas A&M. He is currently Technical Director at Blue Sky Studios in New York, where he wiles away countless hours in a dark cubicle in front of a glowing CRT.
