nabeel shahzad
M.S. Student, Computer Science
nskhan cse.tamu.edu

I hold a M.S. in computer science resulting in my work at the Interface Ecology Lab, which was supervised by Dr. Andruid Kerne. My research interests have evolved from human computer interaction to mixed reality applications and game mechanics. My main focus of research is working with sensors that provide information about the environment and seamless integration of this information into applications that enhance user experience.

I am a principal author of S.IM.PL de/serialization, and the foundation S.IM.PL cross-platform type system.

I developed the iPhone version of the teaching Team Coordination Game. TeC is a mixed-reality game in which we investigate real-life coordination practices and integrate findings into game design.
