Ajit Jain

I am an M.S. student in Computer Science at TAMU doing research in the area of information interfaces under Dr. Andruid Kerne at Interface Ecology lab. My research interests include information visualization, interaction design, sensemaking, context-aware computing, and web semantics.
My research work involves studying, understanding, and advancing creativity support tools, i.e. innovation for innovation. We strive to make people’s engagement with information more interactive, enjoyable, and efficient.

As a part of my Masters thesis, I developed TweetBubble, a free and open source Chrome extension that enables Twitter users to expand @usernames, #hashtags without tabs or windows. Previously, I worked on BigSemanticsService, which is a RESTful interface to the Meta-Metadata structured semantics. I also participated in the development of PhotoNav, a wearable computing app for assisting pedestrian navigation.

Prior to joining Interface Ecology lab, I was working with Samsung Electronics, for a period of 4 years, on Java Virtual Machine graphics and notebook apps. I completed my undergraduate degree in Information Technology from Delhi College of Engineering in 2007.
