Sometimes Eclipse+CVS gets confused about which of your files are really changed when you do a synchronize. The problem is that your timestamps are somehow later than those in the repository. There is a solution, called, Clean Timestamps. It is available from the right-click context menu in the Synchronize view. You must have Eclipse 3.0.1 (or greater); 3.0.0, which we have installed many times, doesn't have this feature.
